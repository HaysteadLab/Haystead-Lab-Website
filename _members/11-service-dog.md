Data is an honorary member of the Haystead Lab and Victoria’s service dog. He proudly holds honorary degrees in physics and medical physics and has a particular talent for kindly asking lab members for treats. In his free time, Data enjoys greeting those that stop by the lab, making new friends, chasing squirrels, and playing with tennis balls.
